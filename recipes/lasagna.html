Lasagna

A classic Italian dish of pasta sheets layered with fillings and baked.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ground Beef
  • Sausage
  • Mozzarella
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Onion
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. In a large pot of salted water boil the lasanga noodles.
  2. Cook the sasuage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce and simmer it for a few minutes to thicken
  3. Stir the ricotta, mozzarella and parmesan cheese together in a bowl.
  4. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
