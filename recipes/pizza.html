Pizza

A baked Italian dish of flatten bread dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese and other Ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Mozzarella
  • Optional: Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees for 30 minutes.
  2. Place your ball of dough in the center of your work surface and use your hands to press it down starting from the center.
  3. Continue patting and stretching the dough until it about 12 inches across. Place dough on cooking surface.
  4. Drizzle a bit of olive oil over the top of your dough and use your hands to spead it evenly over the surface.
  5. Evenly spread pizza sauce over flatten dough, leaving the edges untouched for crust.
  6. Place slices of mozzarella over the sauce.
  7. Optional: Place slices of pepperoni over the sauce.
  8. Place the pizza in the oven and let it bake for 10 minutes or until the dough is cooked.
