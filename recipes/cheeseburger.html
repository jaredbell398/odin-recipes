Cheeseburger

A sandwhich including a beef patty with a slice of melted cheese on top. Condiments include lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, ketchup and mustard.

Ingredients

  • Beef Patty
  • Bun
  • American Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. In a skillet cook the beef patty until it's medium well.
  2. Reduce heat and place a slice of American cheese on top of the patty until it's melted.
  3. Remove patty from skillet and place on bottom bun.
  4. Add a slice of onion and tomato to the top of the patty.
  5. Place a piece of lettuce and a couple pickles on top.
  6. Add ketchup and mustard to the top of the stack.
  7. Place top bun to complete the sandwhich.
